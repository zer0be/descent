Welcome to my unofficial quick mobile campaign tracker (Alpha Testing).

The tracker will not only make it easier for your party members to keep track of their progress, but it will also give you stats on which Heroes get picked most, which dungeons do Heroes lose most in, favorite skills and weapons selected, etc etc. Hopefully this will help answer a lot of the common forum questions of who wins more and which dungeons and expansions are more popular with some real numbers from players. The more groups that use the site the better the statistics will eventually be!

Currently the site is being reworked but soon we will be offering you a preview the campaign tracker.
